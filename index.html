Our Process & Mission

We believe in a transparent and personal, straight-forward approach to car sales. Our process begins with a careful selection of vehicles, followed by a detailed 83-point inspection to ensure they meet our high standards. Once a car is in our inventory, we prepare it for sale, addressing any mechanical needs - in fact we put an average of $2000-$4000 into our reconditioning process to make sure these vehicles are roadworthy as they can be. On the back end, we also offer a 3-year/36000 mile service contract that covers our customers on a barrage of different parts and pieces including, but not limited to engine, transmission, front and rear wheel drive and select electrical parts Our mission is to connect great cars with great people, building trust one sale at a time.
